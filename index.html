About

Jimmy Butterfield is a creative writing student with a passion for fiction, life writing, and digital storytelling. His work is interested in storytelling that is personal, reflective, and attentive to the complexities of everyday experience.

Through his writing, Jimmy explores voice, memory, humour, identity, and the emotional textures of lived experience. He is particularly drawn to the ways fiction, life writing, and digital forms can overlap, creating narratives that feel both intimate and playful.
