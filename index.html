Jimmy Butterfield

Author

Jimmy Butterfield is a creative writing student with a passion for fiction and life writing. His work is interested in storytelling that is personal, reflective, and attentive to the complexities of everyday experience. Through his writing, he explores voice, memory, and the emotional textures of lived experience. Jimmy is particularly drawn to the ways fiction and life writing can overlap, creating narratives that feel both intimate and resonant.
